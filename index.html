Hi! My name is Evan Keith, but I go by the name Fish Slappa across my different creative projects. I'm a musician and writer, as well as a student of linguistics. Most of what you'll find here is in some way related to either music or linguistics.

On the musical side, you'll find links to past performances I've done and music I've made, as well as hopefully some more conceptual musico-technological works and audio software in the future. Most of the music I make is strictly electronic, and grounded in DIY and open-source techniques.

On the linguistics side, I'll post links to articles and research pieces I've written, as well as translations of existing works by other authors. I am particularly interested in historical linguistics and dialect studies related to the Austronesian language family, and much of what I write and translate relates to languages within this family, in particular Indonesian and Javanese.

This website is intended as a sort of CV, collating the projects I've done in a number of different fields in one place for anyone interested to see.
