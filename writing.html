Jalan Raya Pos (Great Post Road) - English Translated Subtitles

Jalan Raya Pos, or The Great Post Road, is an Indonesian documentary film featuring author and essayist Pramoedya Ananta Toer. It recounts the history of the construction of the titular road across the island of Java by the Dutch colonial government. Much of the script is taken from an earlier essay by Pramoedya, of the same name. The film was originally released in Indonesian with Dutch subtitles. I have uploaded the film in full with English subtitles, as I do not believe any others exist. The subtitles are based on my own transcription and translation of the dialogue, so some inaccuracies may have occurred.
